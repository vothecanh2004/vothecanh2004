# "Meet Up Event" Report

### Event Purposes

- Understand yourself better and overcome the barrier of procrastination
- Share knowledge about Hackathons
- Discover ways to build and gain confidence
- Develop a systems thinking mindset

### Speaker Lineup
- **Mr. Uy** - The Iceberg of Procrastination
- **Mr. Linh** - Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci
- **The Ballers Team** - HACKATHON  
- **Ms. Quynh** - Why We Always Need Confidence
- **Mr. Quan** - The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster


### Key Highlights
#### Mr. Uy - The Iceberg of Procrastination
- The root causes of procrastination
- The layers of fear underneath procrastination
- Fear of not being good enough
- Fear of judgment
- Fear of failure
- Solutions provided through the 5-Minute Rule
#### Mr. Linh - Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci
- The unexpected costs incurred when resources are used incorrectly
- Guidelines on practicing AWS effectively without worrying about expenses or forgetting to delete resources
- An introduction to supporting tools such as AWS Cloud Quest and Floci
- A recommended learning roadmap
#### The Ballers Team - HACKATHON  
- Showcasing typical Hackathon projects
- Sharing insights on ongoing and upcoming competitions
- Lessons learned from personal experiences and teamwork

#### Ms. Quynh - Why We Always Need Confidence
- Definition of confidence
- Understanding how confidence manifests in studying and working
- Barriers to maintaining confidence
- Actionable ways to build confidence
- Practical tips to keep your confidence high
#### Mr. Quan - The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster
- Presenting the iceberg model of a software project
- Core values and concepts of DevOps
- Essential principles of DevOps
