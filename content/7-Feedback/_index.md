### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment** The workspace is highly open, and the FCAJ members and peers are incredibly friendly and dynamic. Everyone in the team is always willing to help and support whenever challenges arise, even outside of official working hours. The workplace is consistently kept neat and tidy, creating a comfortable atmosphere that enhances focus and productivity. To foster even stronger bonds, the company might consider organizing additional gathering sessions to tighten solidarity among members.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin** I received dedicated guidance from my Mentor, who always provided detailed instructions, thoroughly explained areas I found unclear, and constantly encouraged me to ask questions. The Admin team was also highly responsive in handling administrative procedures and providing necessary documents, creating the best possible conditions for my work. What I appreciate the most is that my Mentor always provided guidance and allowed me to explore and solve problems independently instead of just giving away the answers.

**3. Alignment Between Work and Academic Major** The assigned tasks within the company are highly compatible with the foundational Information Technology knowledge I accumulated at university. At the same time, the work has opened up many new areas of knowledge that I had never encountered before. This combination helped me both consolidate my theoretical foundation and quickly adapt to practical industry skills.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development** The internship period has provided me with significant opportunities to enhance both my soft skills and professional expertise, from getting familiar with project management tools and optimizing teamwork skills to practicing professional communication in a corporate environment. The real-world experiences shared by my Mentor have also helped me better shape my future career path.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit** The company culture carries a very positive energy: everyone respects and listens to one another, maintaining professionalism in their work while remaining joyful and approachable. Especially during peak project periods, the team spirit shines brightly as everyone stands together to share the pressure, regardless of position or hierarchy. This makes an intern like me feel truly welcomed and an integral part of the collective.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns** The company provides a clear internship allowance policy and always offers flexible scheduling options to help me balance my studies and work. Furthermore, granting interns the opportunity to participate in internal training and sharing sessions is an incredibly valuable asset.


### Other Questions
- What are you **most satisfied with** during your internship?  
 What I am most satisfied with is the dedicated support from my Mentor, the FCAJ members, and the excellent team spirit within the company.
- What do you think the company **needs to improve** for future interns?  
 The company could organize more short gathering or small-scale networking sessions to help members bond with each other more quickly.
- If recommending to friends, would you **advise them to intern here**? Why?  
 I will definitely recommend and encourage my friends to apply for an internship here. This is because FCAJ possesses a highly ideal working environment for students, with actual tasks that closely align with our major.
